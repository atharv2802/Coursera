Tarkarli is a village in Malvan taluka in Sindhudurg district of Maharashtra. It is a beach destination and remote place in southern Maharashtra. Few years ago, Tarkarli beach is declared as a Queen beach of Konkan region.

Tarkarli is 8km south of Malvan and 546km from Mumbai and 410km from Pune on the west coast pf India, at the influence of the Karli river and the Arabian sea.

Our hotel and restaurants are very well-known in Tarkarli. You can reach us by following the given address once you reach Tarkarli.

You can reach Tarkarli by:

BY AIR :

The nearest airport is Dabolim Airport in Goa. From the airport, Tarkarli is 78 km which can be trvalled by car or taxi.

BY RAIL :

By rail, Tarkarli can be accessed from Sindhudurg and also from Kudal and Kankavli by the Konkan Railway.

BY CAR :

Tarkarli is easily accessible by road from Malvan by bus and rickshaw. Tarkarli is 475 km (Panvel Kochi Road, NH 17) and 576 km (Mumbai Kohlapur Road NH 4) away from Mumbai. Daily private, luxury and State Transport bus service available from various parts of Goa, Mumbai, Pune to Malvan.
